# How to Filter Meeting Invitations in Outlook Web

To filter meeting invitations into a specific folder in Outlook Web, follow these simple steps.

## Option 1: The Simplest Steps (Manual Setup)

1.  **Open Settings**:
    *   Go to [outlook.office.com](https://outlook.office.com).
    *   Click the **Gear icon (Settings)** in the top right corner.

2.  **Navigate to Rules**:
    *   Go to **Mail** > **Rules**.
    *   Click **+ Add new rule**.

3.  **Name your rule**:
    *   Type something like `Meeting Invites`.

4.  **Add a Condition**:
    *   Open the dropdown and select **Type**.
    *   In the second dropdown, select **Invitation**.

5.  **Add an Action**:
    *   Select **Move to**.
    *   Click **Select a folder** and choose **New folder**.
    *   Type `Calendar Invites` (or your preferred name) and press **Enter**.

6.  **Save**:
    *   Click **Save** at the bottom.
